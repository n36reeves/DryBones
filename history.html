An Incomplete History of Dry Bones

1988 - Super Mario Bros. 3 (Japan)

  • Dry Bones first appeared in level 1-5, the fortress level.
  • Super Mario Bros. 3 marks the only appearance of Dry Bones on the NES
  • In SMB3, Dry Bones is immune to everything except the star and hammer suit.

1990 - Super Mario World

  • Dry Bones got a bit smarter in this game and no longer walk off ledges.
  • This is the first time we see Dry Bones sport pink Shoes!
  • In SMW, Dry Bones appear larger than Koopa Troopas. How odd.

2006 - New Super Mario Bros.

  • It has been so long since I have seen you my dear.
  • This game features a Dry Bones variant, Super Dry Bones.
  • Dry Bones lets out a cackle when defeating Mario or Luigi.

2023 - Yup, He's Still Dancing

I want more Dry Bones!
